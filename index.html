Our temple

A home for the soul in the heart of Naperville

ISKCON Naperville has served the local community since 1977, offering a sanctuary for spiritual practice, education, and celebration of Vedic culture. Our in-temple shop has long been a way for devotees and visitors to bring a piece of that devotion home.

"We have made this shop available online so that everyone — near and far — can access sacred items and support the mission of spreading Krishna consciousness."

Gift shop

Browse our offerings

About donations: All items in our online shop are offered on a voluntary donation basis, consistent with our in-temple shop. No sales tax is applied. Your generosity directly supports the programs, festivals, and outreach of ISKCON Naperville. Donations are processed securely through PayPal.

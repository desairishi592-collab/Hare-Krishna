Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Hare Krishna Gift Shop online store operated by ISKCON Naperville (Radha Damodar Temple). It is consistent with the privacy policy of our main temple website at iskconnaperville.org.

Privacy Policy

ISKCON Naperville (the "Temple") is committed to protecting the privacy of our donors, visitors, and community members. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use this online gift shop.

About Our Donation Model

All transactions on this website are voluntary donations to ISKCON Naperville, a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit religious organization. Items listed in this shop have a fixed donation amount. This model is consistent with our in-temple shop. No sales tax is collected, as these are donations to a nonprofit religious organization and not commercial sales. All proceeds go directly toward temple programs, outreach, and spiritual services — not for profit.

Information We Collect

When you make a donation through this site, your payment is processed by PayPal. We may receive the following information:

  • Your name and email address (as provided to PayPal)
  • The amount and purpose of your donation
  • Contact information you submit through our contact form

We do not store your credit card or payment information. All payment processing is handled securely by PayPal.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Process and acknowledge your donation
  • Coordinate item pickup or delivery
  • Send donation receipts or acknowledgments
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Send temple newsletters or updates, if you have opted in

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Donor Privacy

ISKCON Naperville respects the privacy of our donors. Donor information is kept confidential and is not shared with outside organizations except as required by law or as necessary to process your donation (e.g., PayPal). We will not publish donor names without explicit permission.

Email Communications

If you register for our email communications, you may receive updates about temple events, programs, and news. You may unsubscribe at any time by following the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us at info@iskconnaperville.org.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics. PayPal may use cookies in the course of processing your donation; please refer to PayPal's Privacy Policy for details.

Third-Party Services

Donations are processed through PayPal. When you click "Donate with PayPal," you will be directed to PayPal's website. PayPal's privacy practices are governed by their own privacy policy. We are not responsible for PayPal's privacy practices.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to exercise caution when submitting personal information online.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us:

  • Address: 1505 McDowell Road, Naperville, IL 60563
  • Phone: 630-608-0209
  • Email: info@iskconnaperville.org

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

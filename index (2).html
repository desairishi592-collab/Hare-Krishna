Our Temple

About ISKCON Naperville

🪷

Who we are

A home for the soul in the heart of Naperville

ISKCON Naperville — the Radha Damodar Temple — has served the local community since 1966, offering a sanctuary for spiritual practice, education, and celebration of Vedic culture. Founded as part of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness by His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada, our temple continues his mission of sharing Krishna consciousness with all.

"We have made this shop available online so that everyone — near and far — can access sacred items and support the mission of spreading Krishna consciousness."

Visit the main temple website

Our Mission

Serving the community through devotion

Spiritual Education

We offer regular classes on Bhagavad Gita, Srimad Bhagavatam, and Vedic philosophy, open to all.

Festival Celebrations

Janmashtami, Ratha Yatra, Diwali, and many more — our festivals bring the community together in joy.

Food Distribution

Prasadam distribution is a cornerstone of our service — offering sanctified food to all who visit.

Community Outreach

We actively engage with the broader Naperville community through events, volunteering, and cultural programs.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Why is it called a "donation" instead of a purchase?

ISKCON Naperville is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit religious organization. Consistent with our in-temple shop, all transactions are classified as voluntary donations to the temple. No sales tax is collected, and all proceeds go directly toward temple programs and outreach, not for profit.

Is there sales tax?

No. Because all transactions are donations to a registered nonprofit religious organization, no Illinois sales tax is applied — consistent with our in-temple shop policy.

How do I receive my item?

After completing your donation, please contact us via the Contact page or visit the temple during open hours to arrange pickup. We are located at 1505 McDowell Road, Naperville, IL 60563.

Is my payment secure?

Yes. All donations are processed through PayPal, a trusted and secure payment platform. We never store your payment information.

Can I donate more than the listed amount?

Absolutely. The amounts listed are fixed donation amounts for each item. If you wish to make an additional offering to the temple, you are always welcome to do so.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Hare Krishna Gift Shop online store operated by ISKCON Naperville (Radha Damodar Temple). It is consistent with the privacy policy of our main temple website at iskconnaperville.org.

Privacy Policy

ISKCON Naperville (the "Temple") is committed to protecting the privacy of our donors, visitors, and community members. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use this online gift shop.

About Our Donation Model

All transactions on this website are voluntary donations to ISKCON Naperville, a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit religious organization. Items listed in this shop have a fixed donation amount. This model is consistent with our in-temple shop. No sales tax is collected, as these are donations to a nonprofit religious organization and not commercial sales. All proceeds go directly toward temple programs, outreach, and spiritual services — not for profit.

Information We Collect

When you make a donation through this site, your payment is processed by PayPal. We may receive:

  • Your name and email address (as provided to PayPal)
  • The amount and purpose of your donation
  • Contact information you submit through our contact form

We do not store your credit card or payment information. All payment processing is handled securely by PayPal.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Process and acknowledge your donation
  • Coordinate item pickup or delivery
  • Send donation receipts or acknowledgments
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Send temple newsletters or updates, if you have opted in

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Donor Privacy

ISKCON Naperville respects the privacy of our donors. Donor information is kept confidential and is not shared with outside organizations except as required by law or as necessary to process your donation. We will not publish donor names without explicit permission.

Email Communications

If you register for our email communications, you may receive updates about temple events, programs, and news. You may unsubscribe at any time by contacting us at info@iskconnaperville.org.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use tracking cookies or analytics. PayPal may use cookies in the course of processing your donation; please refer to PayPal's Privacy Policy for details.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage caution when submitting personal information online.
